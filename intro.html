Early Years

I was born in Providence, Rhode Island as the eldest of three children. My mother and father bought a house in Pawtucket, R.I.. My sister and brother are four and nine years younger than me, respectively. We had a cocker spaniel named Buffie who lived to be nineteen years old!

Education

I went to a Montessori school until 5th grade then to an all girls catholic K-12 school. I obtained a B.S. in chemistry at Providence College, Providence, RI. I then went to medical school at Lewis Katz School of Medicine at Temple University in Philadelphia, PA and obtained a doctorate in pharmacology. Now, I am finishing my Master’s of Science degree in Education at Quinnipiac University (QU) and this is my last summer at QU. I am enrolled at Temple University Fall 2023-25 in School of Education to learn to teach medical students the subject of pharmacology in the Professional Health Education program.

Work Experience

As a bench scientist, and a pharmacologist, I worked at National Eye Institute in Bethesda, Maryland in Proteomics. Also, I was a bench scientist at the US Army Medical Institute of Chemical Defense at Aberdeen Proving Grounds, Edgewood, Maryland in the neuropharmacology and neurotoxicology labs on scientific teams. Now, in my future, I am going to teach (face-face, online, and hybrid) pharmacology in a health sciences or medical school after I finish my master’s degree at Temple University in 2025.

Family Life

I am a single mother residing in Connecticut and have two adult children who are in college in CT. I love to go to the gym, read, volunteer at my local church, play sports, spend time outside, and am a third order Lay person in the Order of Preachers in the Catholic Church.
