Our Family Recipes

My grandmother taught my mother how to cook Syrian and Greek food recipes. These recipes are family traditions. Our favorite food in the summer is a BBQ steak, Greek Chicken Salad, corn on the cob, and ice cream for dessert. Here is our family traditional recipe called: Greek Chicken Salad.

  • 3 1/2 cups cooked chopped chicken
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1/3 cup sliced kalamata olives
  • 1 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp. chopped fresh dill
  1. Place chicken in a large bowl
  2. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, yogurt, lemon juice, feta, salt and pepper
  3. Stir mayonnaise mixture into chicken
  4. Cut other vegetables & Stir in remaining ingredients
  5. Season to taste with salt
  6. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving
